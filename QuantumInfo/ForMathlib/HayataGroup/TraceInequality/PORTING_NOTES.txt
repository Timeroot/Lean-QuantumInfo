When porting the code from https://github.com/Hayata-Yamasaki-Group/lean-quantum to this repo,
besides moving the code to a different directory (and thus editing the imports at the tops of
files), three changes were made:
 * Some of the `Nontrivial` and `CompleteSpace` instance variables were moved around or `omit`ted, in
   or to prevent the linter warnings about unneeded variables. This was done in:
   - BlockDiagonal.lean
   - GeneralizedPerspectiveFunction.lean
   - HilbertSchmidtOperatorSpace.lean
   - JensenOperatorInequality.lean
   - JensenOperatorInequalityIImpIV.lean
   - JensenOperatorInequalityIVtoV.lean
   - LiebAndoTrace.lean
 * Three instances of `set_option backward.isDefEq.respectTransparency false` in LiebAndoTrace
   were removed, since these are a new thing with v4.29.
 * In BlockDiagonal.lean, in `blockDiagonalHom` in the proof of `commutes'`, there was a final
   `rfl` tactic that was needed in v4.29 but not in v4.28, probably for reason related to
   respectTransparency. This final line was commented out.
